South India — coffee hills, wildlife and green calm.

The south from Bengaluru — the coffee country of Coorg and Chikmagalur, the wildlife of Kabini, and the tea hills of Munnar. Lush, restful and great for a team that needs to slow down and reconnect.

Why here

Green, calm, and quietly restorative.

South India is where teams go to breathe. The plantations of Coorg and Chikmagalur, the forests and wildlife of Kabini, the rolling tea estates of Munnar — it’s lush, unhurried country that’s perfect for offsites built around reconnection and reset, not just adrenaline.

Easy to reach from Bengaluru, it’s a natural choice for the south’s big tech and GCC teams.

What’s here

The pull of the place.

  • Coffee country. Coorg and Chikmagalur — plantation stays, misty hills, real calm.
  • Wildlife. Kabini for safaris and forest lodges, a memorable team backdrop.
  • Tea hills. Munnar’s green slopes for a restful, scenic offsite.
  • Close to Bengaluru. Convenient for the south’s tech and GCC teams.
